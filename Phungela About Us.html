About Us

Phungela Online Solutions is an online solutions provider that aims to provide business owners from all walks of life an opportunity to take advantage of the global market of customers world-wide by representing their business online with a high quality, fully functional and secure ecommerce website.

It was founded by a web design enthusiast who goes by the name of Ntsikelelo Mnqokoyi (pictured) who realised the importance of a website for businesses after seeing first-hand how powerful a website can be especially for business owners who want to sell their goods/services to the entire world, although a website will also work wonders for business owners who would only like to increase sales within their local region of operation.

The name Phungela is derived from our founder's favorite ancestral clan name from his family's lineage

Phungela Online Solutions will make sure that your business has a website that your business and customers can be proud of so why don't you contact us and find out more about this amazing opportunity.
